About

I am a PhD Candidate at Leiden University studying computational chemistry.

My work focuses on atomistic simulations of electrochemical interfaces and the microscopic behavior of water and ions near surfaces.

I am mainly interested in:

  • Electrical Double Layer (EDL)
  • Molecular Dynamics (MD) simulations
  • Density Functional Theory (DFT)
  • Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM)
