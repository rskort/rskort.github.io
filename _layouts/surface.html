Cell and site definitions

{{ surface.math_summary }}

Positions in a periodic surface cell can be written as \(\mathbf{r}=u\mathbf{a}_1+v\mathbf{a}_2+z\hat{\mathbf{n}}\). An ontop site shares the lateral \((u,v)\) coordinates of a surface atom; bridge and hollow sites are defined by the appropriate midpoints or polygon centres. Coordination here is an ideal geometric count, not a statement about bond order.
