Mathematics

Math is rendered with MathJax. Inline math works like this: the quadratic formula is $x = \dfrac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$.

Display equations are also supported:

$$ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2}\, dx = \sqrt{\pi} $$

Another example — Euler's identity:

$$ e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0 $$

And the definition of the Fourier transform:

$$ \hat{f}(\xi) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x)\, e^{-2\pi i x \xi}\, dx $$
